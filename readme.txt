Allows easy integration of Westgate Partners and your WordPress website.

== Description ==

This plugin allows you to utilize the WordPress Shortcodes from the Westgate Partners website. You can easily add your affiliate banners and widgets right into your visual editor.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `westgate-partners.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Add your Westgate Partners "WordPress Shortcode" to your posts or pages
